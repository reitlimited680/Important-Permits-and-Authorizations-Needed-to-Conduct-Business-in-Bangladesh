## 1. RJSC Business Registration

In Bangladesh, the main body in charge of registering businesses, partnerships, and societies is the Registrar of Joint Stock Companies and Firms (RJSC).

### Relevant Business Forms

- Partnership Firm
- Private Limited Company
- Public Limited Company
- Society

### Why It’s Important

A company can open corporate bank accounts, enter into contracts, raise capital, and safeguard shareholders' rights after registering as an RJSC.

A company cannot lawfully function as a corporate entity without RJSC registration.

---

## 2. Local Authorities’ Trade License

All companies operating in Bangladesh, regardless of their size or industry, must have a trade license. In urban areas, it is issued by city corporations; in rural areas, it is issued by union parishads or municipalities.

### Important Details Required

- Owner or director details
- Business address
- Type of business

Trade licenses are evidence that a company is allowed to operate in a particular jurisdiction and must be renewed every year.

---

## 3. TIN (Tax Identification Number)

The National Board of Revenue (NBR) requires each business entity and individual entrepreneur to obtain a Tax Identification Number (TIN).

### The Significance of TIN

- Applying for government approvals
- Opening bank accounts
- Filing income tax returns
- Taking part in tenders

Now that TIN registration is done digitally, compliance is quicker and more open.

---

## 4. BIN (Business Identification Number) / VAT Registration

Companies that supply taxable goods or services are required to register for Value Added Tax (VAT) and receive a Business Identification Number (BIN) from the NBR.

### Who Must Register for VAT?

Manufacturers: Exporters & Importers and service providers who surpass turnover limits.

For legal invoicing and to avoid fines during audits, VAT compliance is crucial.

---

## 5. Import & Export Registration Certificates (IRC and ERC)

Businesses that trade internationally are required to obtain:

- ERC for exporting goods
- IRC for importing goods

The Office of the Chief Controller of Imports and Exports (CCI&E) is responsible for issuing these certificates.

### Principal Advantages

- The ability to legally clear customs
- Transactions involving foreign currencies and involvement in international trade

---

## 6. Approval from the Bangladesh Investment Development Authority (BIDA)

Large-scale local projects and foreign investors frequently need the Bangladesh Investment Development Authority's (BIDA) approval.

Approvals for foreign direct investment (FDI) work permits for foreign nationals. The distribution of industrial plots and utility connections are all facilitated by BIDA.

As a one-stop shop, BIDA greatly streamlines the investment process.

---

## 7. Sector-Specific Licenses and Regulatory Approvals

Specialized regulators must grant additional approvals for some industries.

Examples include:

- Bangladesh Bank: Fintech firms and financial institutions
- Bangladesh Telecommunication Regulatory Commission (BTRC): ISPs and telecom companies
- Drug Administration Directorate General (DGDA): Drugs and medical equipment
- Environmental Clearance Certificates from the Department of Environment (DoE)

If sector-specific licenses are not obtained, there may be penalties, suspension, or closure.

---

## 8. Environmental Clearance Certificate (ECC)

Businesses must obtain an Environmental Clearance Certificate from the Department of Environment (DoE), particularly for manufacturing and industrial projects.

### Classifications

- Orange-A, Red (high impact)
- Orange-B, Green (low impact)

Renewal is required on a regular basis, and the level of scrutiny rises with environmental risk.

---

## 9. Building and Fire Safety Approvals

The Fire Service and Civil Defense Authority must grant approval for offices, factories, and commercial buildings.

### Goal

- Compliance with fire safety regulations
- Preparation for emergency exits
- Assurance of worker safety

For factories, warehouses, and high-rise office buildings, this approval is especially important.
